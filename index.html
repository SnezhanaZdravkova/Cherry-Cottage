Welcome to Cherry Cottage


We are a family run farm located outside Dublin.

We love to have visitors and you can see how our poultry farm is run, and perhaps buy some of our delicious, nutritious eggs to take home with you.

With the popularity of electric cars, you will be happy to learn that we also have charging points available so you can charge your car while you wait.


Charging Point:

We have charging available for all common electric vehicles - all you need is to park your car, plug in, and enjoy a cup of delicious coffee while you wait.

Please note that a credit card is needed when you begin charging.


Free parking space


Free high quality coffee for all customers


Large green area for walk or relax while your car is pluged in.

About us


Cherry Cottage is a lovely place, based along Old Dublin Road. Easy to park on the free parking spaces provided.
